Top Destinations in Sri Lanka

From golden beaches to ancient cities, misty hills to wildlife jungles Sri Lanka is a treasure trove of unforgettable experiences. At Ceylon Travels, we bring you the island’s most iconic and breathtaking destinations.



Yala


Explore Sri Lanka’s most famous national park, home to leopards, elephants, and wild beauty.

Bentota


A paradise for beach lovers with golden sands, river cruises, and thrilling water sports.

Mirissa


A vibrant coastal town known for whale watching, surfing, and magical sunsets.

Udawalawe


Ideal for elephant safaris and close encounters with wild animals in open landscapes.

Unawatuna


A calm, crescent-shaped bay perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and relaxing.

Sinharaja Rainforest


A lush tropical rainforest and UNESCO site rich in biodiversity and nature trails.

Nuwara Eliya


Nicknamed “Little England,” it’s a cool hill country retreat with tea estates and colonial charm.

Ella


scenic town famous for hikes, waterfalls, and the iconic Nine Arches Bridge.

Horton Plains


A highland plateau offering surreal views, misty grasslands, and the famous World’s End cliff.

Hikkaduwa


A lively beach town with coral reefs, nightlife, and great surf.

Dambulla


Home to Sri Lanka’s largest and best-preserved cave temple complex.

Anuradhapura


An ancient capital with massive stupas and sacred Bodhi Tree — rich in history and spirituality.

Sigiriya


Climb the iconic Lion Rock Fortress, a UNESCO site with stunning views and history.

Kandy


A scenic city known for the Temple of the Tooth, cultural shows, and serene lake views.

Colombo


Sri Lanka’s modern capital, blending colonial history with shopping, dining, and nightlife.

Galle


Discover colonial architecture, boutiques, and sea views inside the iconic Galle Fort.

Arugam Bay


A surfer’s paradise with laid-back vibes and one of the world’s top surf breaks.
